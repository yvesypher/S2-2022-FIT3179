120 years of Nobel Prize

Established in 1901, Nobel Prize is a prestigious annual award
for outstanding achievement in such fields as Physics, Chemistry,
Physiology or Medicine, Literature, Peace, and Economic Science (from 1968).
The Nobel Foundation was founded on the will of Alfred Nobel.

USA dominates the world in Nobel Prize

These figures illustrate the number of Nobel winners among top five countries. On the whole, these top 5 countries account for more than half of the number of Nobel winners worldwide. This is reasonable as these are the world leading countries with long-term history in science, social, and economic development. It is noticeable that USA takes up more than a quarter of the worldwide figure. It leads the world in the number of Nobel laureates, followed by United Kingdom, Germany, France, and Sweeden. The gap between USA and these European countries are considerably huge. USA outweights other countries in not only the total number of Nobel laureates but also in each category.

Nobel prize can be shared

A Nobel prize can be shared up to three individuals. However, Nobel Peace Prize can be awarded to organisations of more than three people. Nobel Prizes are not awarded posthumously. In case a person is awarded but dies before receiving it, the prize is still presented. It is observable that Physiology or Medicine category has the highest number of Nobel laureates overall and shared by three laureates. Meanwhile, Literature category has the most individual laureate and no three-shared prize awardees.

Monetary award can make a Nobel laureate a millionaire

Money prize awarded to Nobel winners are taken from Nobel Foundation. In 1900, this fund is established by Alfred Nobel's personal fortune. He bequeathed 94% of his total asset, which is 31 million Swedish kronor (approximately US$ 280 million in 2021). Owing to the exemption from all taxes in Sweeden since 1946 and from investment taxes in USA since 1953, the foundation's investments have become more profitable. Nobel laureates are awarded a sum of money when they receive their prizes. Moreover, the money prize is divided if there is prize share. If two laureates share the prize in a category, the award grant is divided equally between the recipients. If there are three, the awarding committee has the option of dividing the grant equally, or awarding one-half to one recipient and one-quarter to each of the others.
